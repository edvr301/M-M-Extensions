OUR STORY

M&M Extensions

M&M Extensions are an arm of M&M Perfect Solutions – building contractors on a mission to find our clients' perfect, tailored building solutions. Along our journey to search for the best building practices, we discovered SIPs, and we quickly saw the power they had in transforming the efficiency, quality and costs of our building solutions.

Our work always aims to deliver design-focused extensions and renovations that remain inherently true to our customers' desires SIPs exceed every category in delivering this outcome.

Why Did We Choose SIPs?

SIPs are the cost-effective, sustainable-focused building solution for savvy, modern homeowners. We can create precisely measured panels off-site and install them in a matter of days. Once installed, SIPs provide unrivalled thermal insulation and airtightness, reducing your ongoing energy costs.
