Why Choose Sips?

Engineered and constructed to suit any building, we can create SIPs that completely align to your architectural and design vision, without compromising on appearance, to create a space that you can call your own.

SIPs panels increase the thermal insulation of your home by over 100%, compared to standard building methods; they also have an excellent airtightness which results in low energy building standards.

SIPs are among the most sustainable and eco-friendly building systems available today. Not only do SIPs heavily reduce energy consumption in the home, they also reduce CO2 levels in the environment, use considerably less energy during the production stages and the factory construction method results in less wasted resources compared to on-site construction.

Manufacturing your unique SIP floors and walls offsite results in installation that is both rapid and cost-effective. The process enables a streamlined and effortless operation with little to no delays or complications.

Steps on Building Sips

A Guide to the Process

1. Concept collaboration

We discuss your building extension, design plans, and ultimate desires, collaborating on suggestions and recommendations to arrive at the perfect solution.

2. Initial works

We conduct the measurements, drawings, and plans to execute an efficient work programme.

3. SIPs production

Your panels are produced in ideal factory conditions, with automated machinery to ensure materials are securely joined and cut to precision.

4. Seamless transition

These eco-friendly and sustainable SIPs are quickly installed, eliminating a prolonged building process, which in turn would require you to vacate into offsite accommodation. With our SIPs, not only do you get a quick and customisable building experience but you can wake up in the comfort of your own home.

5. Handing it back too you.

Once your custom built SIP building is complete and fully constructed we will hand it back to you to enjoy.
